1. **The Challenge**
The fashion retail market is saturated. To succeed, the app must bridge the gap between "scrolling for inspiration" and "committing to a purchase" by reducing friction during the account creation and checkout processes.

**2. Key UX Solutions**
Progressive Onboarding: The design uses a clear "Get Started" splash screen followed by simple social login options (Facebook, Google, Apple). This minimizes the "barrier to entry" for new users.

Visual Hierarchy in Search: The "Home" and "Favorites" screens use card-based layouts with clear pricing and high-quality imagery. The use of a "Preview" screen for individual items allows users to inspect details before adding to the cart.

Frictionless Checkout: The checkout flow is broken down into manageable chunks: Shipping -> Payment Method -> Order Summary. The "Order Placed" screen with a "Track Order" CTA provides immediate post-purchase reassurance.

**3. Visual Identity**
Color Palette: #FFFFFF, #BCDDCD and #3A5447. This evokes a sense of "premium organic" or high-end fashion, distinguishing it from the typical bright blues or reds of mass-market apps.

Typography: Clean, sans-serif fonts ensure readability, especially on smaller mobile screens.
